Madrasa Arabiya Islamia

Nurturing faith and knowledge in our community
We are committed to fostering an environment of learning, spirituality, and strong moral character, empowering our students to become knowledgable and confident Muslims.

Our Educational Programmes

We offer a structured and comprehensive curriculum designed to cater to students at every stage of their learning journey, from foundational studies for young children to advanced scholarship.

Foundational Maktab

This program is the foundation of Islamic learning for children. The curriculum is designed to be engaging and progressive, ensuring students can read the Quran and understand their core duties as Muslims.

Learn More 

Hifz-ul-Quran

This is a dedicated program for students who wish to commit the entire Holy Quran to memory. It is a rigorous and highly rewarding path that requires significant dedication from the student and their family.

Learn More 

Advanced Islamic Scholarship

For students who have completed their foundational studies and wish to pursue advanced Islamic scholarship, we offer a multiyear "Aalim" course.

Learn More 

Language Course

As a key part of our charitable objectives, we are proud to offer dedicated classes for Arabic and Urdu at both Basic and Advanced levels.

Learn More
